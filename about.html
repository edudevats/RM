Our Company

RM International Trading Group S.A. de C.V. is a duly incorporated company under the laws of the United Mexican States, operating in full compliance with all applicable fiscal obligations and regulations. We are accredited and authorized to conduct business activities in accordance with the Mexican Commercial Code and relevant tax provisions.

Legal Structure

As a Sociedad Anónima de Capital Variable (S.A. de C.V.), we maintain the highest standards of corporate governance and transparency. Our operations are conducted under the Simplified Trust Regime (Régimen Simplificado de Confianza), ensuring compliance with all tax regulations and providing our clients with confidence in our business practices.

Our Commitment

We are committed to facilitating secure and transparent commercial transactions. Our customer identification sheet serves as verification of our corporate identity, legal standing, and commitment to regulatory compliance. This documentation ensures that all our business partners can conduct transactions with complete confidence in our legitimacy and reliability.
